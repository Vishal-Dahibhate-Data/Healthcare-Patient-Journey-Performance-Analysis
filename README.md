# 🏥 Healthcare Patient Journey & Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Comprehensive Healthcare Analytics Using Power BI, SQL & Excel

A Business Intelligence project designed to analyze patient journeys, hospital operations, treatment outcomes, and healthcare performance metrics. The dashboard provides actionable insights into patient admissions, satisfaction levels, wait times, length of stay, and treatment costs, enabling data-driven decision-making in healthcare management.

## 📌 Project Overview

The Healthcare Patient Journey & Performance Dashboard offers a comprehensive view of patient experiences and hospital performance. By integrating healthcare data and visualizing key operational metrics, the dashboard helps healthcare administrators identify trends, optimize resource allocation, improve patient satisfaction, and enhance overall healthcare delivery.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of patient and operational data, making it challenging to monitor performance and identify areas for improvement.

This project addresses key questions such as:

* What factors influence patient satisfaction?
* How do wait times impact patient experience?
* Which departments incur the highest treatment costs?
* What are the most common admission types?
* How does the length of stay affect healthcare costs?
* What trends exist across patient demographics?

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze patient admission and discharge patterns.
* Evaluate patient satisfaction across different demographics.
* Monitor hospital wait times and treatment efficiency.
* Identify cost drivers across departments.
* Assess patient recovery and referral outcomes.
* Examine relationships between length of stay and treatment costs.
* Build interactive dashboards for healthcare performance monitoring.

## 📈 Key Insights

### Patient Journey Insights

* Emergency admissions account for the majority of patient admissions.
* Most patients are successfully recovered upon discharge.
* Patient satisfaction remains stable across departments.

### Operational Insights

* Average wait time is consistent across departments.
* Longer hospital stays are associated with higher treatment costs.
* Certain departments contribute significantly to overall healthcare expenditure.

### Financial Insights

* Cost patterns vary across departments.
* Resource-intensive treatments drive higher operational costs.
* Monitoring patient stay duration can help optimize expenses.
